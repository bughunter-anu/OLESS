Powerful Features

Everything you need for a seamless online examination experience

Secure Environment

Advanced anti-cheating measures including copy-paste prevention, tab detection, and randomized questions to ensure exam integrity.

Real-time Timer

Synchronized countdown timers with auto-submit functionality. Students never lose track of their remaining time.

Randomized Questions

Each student receives a unique set of questions from the question bank, preventing answer sharing.

Analytics & Reports

Comprehensive analytics with topic-wise strength/weakness analysis and detailed performance reports.

Organized question bank with MCQ and True/False types, categorized by subject, topic, and difficulty.
